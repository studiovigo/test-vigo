Introducing Orion Union

18 DE MARÇO DE 2026
Discover Orion Union — a network where independent poker clubs stay true to their identity while growing within a more stable, connected, and secure ecosystem.
Behind every successful poker club, there is a strong and engaged community. However, across most online poker networks, there is still very little structure connecting those communities in a consistent way. Orion was created to change that by building a private international union where clubs can operate independently while benefiting from a shared, reliable ecosystem. In simple terms, Orion Union is a structured network where selected clubs operate within a shared environment while maintaining their independence. The objective is simple: to provide a clear structure that allows clubs to remain independent while benefiting from a stable and well-organized ecosystem. Each club within Orion maintains its own identity and community. The Union does not replace clubs or centralize their operations. Instead, it provides the underlying structure that allows them to operate more efficiently within a broader and more connected network. It provides union-level operational support across key areas. This includes coordinated promotions, on-demand table setups, and a dedicated security team responsible for maintaining fair play. This blog will serve as a space to explore topics related to the online poker ecosystem, the role of structured networks like Orion, as well as practical tips, operational insights, and best practices for club hosts and agents. Orion is built around a simple belief: independent clubs grow stronger when they are connected through a clear and reliable structure. This is the foundation we are building.
